About PSP Library

Our story, mission, and the passionate team behind Sri Karanpur's most trusted study center.

Our Story

Built for Students,
By a Student at Heart

Perfect Solution Point Reading Library was established on 5 January 2020 in Sri Karanpur, Rajasthan, with one clear goal — to give every competitive exam aspirant a clean, disciplined, and fully equipped environment to study without distractions.

Located at Devta Colony, Ward 24, near Government Hospital, PSP Library has grown into the most trusted study center in the region. With over 1000+ happy students and 5+ years of dedicated service, we remain committed to supporting every student's dream.

Our fully AC reading halls, high-speed WiFi, extended 15-hour daily operations, and unwavering focus on discipline make us the number one choice for students preparing for REET, RAS, RPSC, SSC, Railway, and other competitive exams.

Meet the Founder

The Vision Behind PSP Library

Amar Kamboj
✦ Founder & Owner — Perfect Solution Point Library

Amar Kamboj founded Perfect Solution Point Reading Library with the vision of providing every student in Sri Karanpur access to a world-class study environment at an affordable price. Understanding the real challenges competitive exam aspirants face daily, he created a space built on discipline, cleanliness, and top-class facilities — so students can stay fully focused on their goals without distraction.

Under his dedicated leadership, PSP Library has served over 1000+ students since its inception in January 2020, becoming the most recommended and trusted study center in Sri Karanpur and the surrounding region.
