Our Story

Built for Students,
By a Student at Heart

Perfect Solution Point Reading Library was proudly established on 5 January 2020 in Sri Karanpur, Rajasthan — with one powerful goal: to give every competitive exam aspirant a clean, calm, and fully equipped environment where nothing stands between them and their success.

Conveniently located at Devta Colony, Ward 24, near Government Hospital, Sri Karanpur, PSP has grown into the most trusted study destination in the region, serving 1000+ students over 5+ years of dedicated excellence.

From fully AC halls and high-speed WiFi to 15 hours of daily operation — 7 days a week — PSP Library is purpose-built for students who are serious about REET, RAS, RPSC, SSC, Railway, and all competitive examinations.
