FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining PSP Library.

Can I change my shift after joining?
+
Yes, you can upgrade or switch your shift at the beginning of the next month by speaking directly with the library staff. Upgrades are simple and flexible.
Is the library open on Sundays and public holidays?
+
Yes! PSP Library is open every day of the year — including all Sundays and public holidays — from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We never close so your preparation never stops.
Is there storage for bags and personal belongings?
+
Yes, secure storage facilities are available inside the library so members can safely store their bags and belongings while studying.
How do I pay the monthly membership fee?
+
Monthly membership fees can be paid in cash or via UPI/online transfer at the library counter at the start of each month. Payment is simple and convenient.
Is WiFi available during all shifts?
+
Yes, high-speed WiFi is available throughout all study hours — 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM — for all members regardless of which shift plan they have chosen.
What competitive exams do students prepare for here?
+
PSP Library is ideal for students preparing for REET, RAS, RPSC 2nd Grade, SSC, Railway exams, Patwari, Gram Sevak, Police and various other state and central government competitive examinations.
