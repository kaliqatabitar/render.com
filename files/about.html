Our Story

Born From a Student's Need for Focus

Perfect Solution Point Reading Library was established on January 5, 2020, with a clear and compelling vision: to create the most focused, disciplined, and student-friendly reading environment in Sri Karanpur, Rajasthan.

The founder recognized a critical gap in the educational infrastructure of Sri Karanpur. Thousands of aspirants were preparing for competitive exams — REET, RAS, Patwari, SSC, Banking, Railways — but they lacked a dedicated, quiet, and well-equipped space to study consistently. The distractions of home, the unavailability of quiet public spaces, and the absence of any modern library in the area were constant hurdles.

PSP Library was built to be the answer. Located at Ward No. 24, Devta Colony, behind Government Hospital, Sri Karanpur, it is easily accessible to students from across the town and surrounding villages. From day one, the library was designed with student welfare as the central priority.

Today, more than five years and 1,000+ students later, Perfect Solution Point stands as a testament to what focused dedication and quality infrastructure can achieve. Our students have cleared REET, cracked Patwari exams, qualified for banking jobs, and secured government positions — and they consistently credit the PSP environment as a key factor in their success.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide every student in Sri Karanpur access to a world-class study environment that enables them to achieve their full potential.

🔭 Our Vision

To be the most trusted educational support infrastructure in Rajasthan's Sriganganagar district, empowering a generation of achievers.

💎 Quality First

Amar Kamboj

Founder & Managing Director

With a deep passion for education and community development, Amar Kamboj established Perfect Solution Point in 2020. His vision was simple but powerful: every student in Sri Karanpur deserves a place where they can study without distraction, with access to modern amenities, at an affordable price.

Under his leadership, PSP has grown from a small reading room to Sri Karanpur's most preferred study destination, helping hundreds of students clear competitive exams every year.

Our Journey

January 2020

PSP Library Founded

Perfect Solution Point Reading Library opens its doors in Ward No. 24, Devta Colony, Sri Karanpur, with a mission to serve competitive exam aspirants.

2020–2021

Growing Student Community

Within the first year, over 200 students enrolled, word spread fast across Sri Karanpur about the library's discipline, cleanliness, and AC facility.

2022

High-Speed WiFi & Tech Upgrades

Installed high-speed broadband WiFi throughout the library and upgraded to ergonomic seating and anti-glare LED lighting for better study comfort.

2023

500+ Students Milestone

Celebrated 500+ enrolled students. Multiple alumni cleared REET, Patwari, and Banking exams — validating PSP's impact on competitive exam outcomes.

2024–2025

1000+ Students & Expansion

Reached the 1,000+ student milestone. Expanded seating capacity and added personal locker facilities for all shift students. Continued 4.9★ rating.
