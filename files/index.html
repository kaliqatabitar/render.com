Our Story

Empowering Students Across Sri Karanpur

Perfect Solution Point Reading Library was founded on January 5, 2020 with a singular mission: to provide the students of Sri Karanpur and surrounding areas with a world-class study environment that matches the seriousness of their aspirations.

Established in Ward No. 24, Devta Colony, behind Government Hospital, Sri Karanpur, our library has grown from a modest beginning to become the most trusted and recommended reading space in the region. We understand the unique challenges that competitive exam aspirants face — the need for silence, discipline, consistent study hours, and access to resources.

With over 1,000 students served in just 5 years and a near-perfect satisfaction rating, PSP Library is the definitive answer to the question: "Where should I study in Sri Karanpur?"

Are you a student in Sri Karanpur, Rajasthan, searching for the ideal place to study consistently and achieve your academic or competitive exam goals? Look no further than Perfect Solution Point Reading Library — the most-reviewed, most-trusted, and highest-rated reading library in Sri Karanpur.

Why Students Choose PSP as Their Study Home

The biggest challenge for serious students in Sri Karanpur has always been finding a quiet, distraction-free place to study for long hours. Home environments often come with interruptions, limited space, and noise. Cafés and public spaces lack discipline. That's exactly why Perfect Solution Point was built — to fill this critical gap.

Our fully air-conditioned reading halls are open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, giving you 15 hours of uninterrupted, productive study time. The library is operational all 7 days of the week, including public holidays, ensuring you never lose a study day when it matters most.

Affordable Plans Starting at ₹400/Month

We believe that quality study infrastructure should be accessible to every student, regardless of their financial background. Our pricing is deliberately kept student-friendly: Morning Shift (7 AM–2:30 PM) at ₹400/month, Evening Shift (2:30 PM–10 PM) at ₹400/month, and Full Day Access (7 AM–10 PM) at just ₹700/month. This makes PSP Library one of the most affordable premium reading spaces in Rajasthan.

Location – Easily Accessible in Sri Karanpur
