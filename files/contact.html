FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

📌 What is the monthly fee at PSP Library?

Morning or Evening shift: ₹400/month. Full Day access: ₹700/month. No hidden charges.

📌 Is the library open on Sundays and holidays?

Yes! We are open 7 days a week, 365 days a year — including all public holidays and Sundays — from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

📌 Do I get a personal locker with my membership?

Yes, every enrolled student gets a personal locker to store their books, notes, and study materials securely.

📌 Is WiFi included in all plans?

Yes! High-speed WiFi is available to all students on all shift plans at no extra charge.

📌 How do I enroll at PSP Library?

Simply call us at +91 86999 31293, WhatsApp us, or visit us directly at our Devta Colony address. You can also fill out the contact form above and we'll get back to you quickly.
