Library Code of Conduct

Rules That Protect Your Focus

Our rules exist to ensure every student can study without distraction. Please follow them for the benefit of all members.

1

Strictly Silent Zone

Maintain absolute silence inside the reading hall. Whispering, phone calls, and loud noises are prohibited at all times.

2

Mobile Phones on Silent

All mobile phones must be on silent mode. Headphones are allowed only for online lectures on mute volume.

3

Carry Your ID Card

Students must carry their PSP Library ID card at all times. Entry may be denied without a valid membership card.

4

No Food Inside

Eating inside the reading hall is strictly prohibited. Use the designated break area outside for snacks and meals.

5

Keep Premises Clean

Maintain cleanliness at your seat and in common areas. Dispose of waste in designated bins. Report any issues to management.

6

Respect Fellow Students

Be considerate of others' study time. Any form of disturbance or disrespectful behavior may lead to membership cancellation.

7

Book Your Seat Timely

Arrive on time for your shift. Reserved seats that remain unoccupied for 30+ minutes may be reassigned to waiting students.

8

Monthly Renewal Required

Membership is renewed monthly. Lapsed memberships lose reserved locker and seat preferences. Renew before the 1st of each month.
